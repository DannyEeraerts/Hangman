How to play our Interactive Hangman Game

  • To start the game, click on the button "new game"
  • You will see a number of short underlines together that represent the number of letters in the word or expression you have to guess. (e.g. _ _ _ _ _ _ _ ).
  • A word that contains a hyphen or an expression that contains one or more spaces is already indicated. (e.g. _ _ _ - _ _ _ _ ) or (e.g. _ _ _   _ _ _ _ ).
  • Click on the letter that you think is included in the word.
  • If you guess the correct letter, that letter become green and will appear in the position in the word. It will replace one of the littel lines (e.g. _ _ A _ _ _ _ ).
  • If the letter you guessed is not in the word, the letter will become red and a part of the gallows tree appear in the image.
  • If the body appears, you are 'hanged' which means you have lost. You have eight chances before being hanged.
  • If you are hanged, it is the opponent's turn
  • If you guess all the letters in the word before you are 'hanged', you win.
  • You get the maximum score of 8 points if you guess the word without mistakes.
  • Every error is minus one point.
  • You loose 3 points when you ask a hint. Thus a negative score is possible.
  • You can continue to play as long as you are not hung up. The points are added together.

GOOD LUCK.
